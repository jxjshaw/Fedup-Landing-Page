Explaining the issue with our justice system

Take a look around your social media and the issues are evident: the process is outdated, our enforcers are unaccountable.

Thats why we’re building FedUp: a social network for social justice.

Through community sharing of injustice in our communities, we aim to empower those overlooked by the system, and to give a voice to the voiceless.   Join us.
